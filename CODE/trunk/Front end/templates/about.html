Durian AI is an advanced AI platform engineered for precision agriculture. Leveraging deep convolutional neural networks and real-time object detection, it analyzes durian leaf and fruit images to identify diseases and defects with exceptional accuracy. The system empowers farmers to detect early-stage infections, enabling timely intervention and sustainable crop management. Powered by state-of-the-art models like ResNet, DenseNet, and YOLO, it delivers fast, reliable results on edge devices. Our vision: transform durian farming through intelligent, accessible, and eco-friendly technology for farmers worldwide.
